1. What Wanwun is

Wanwun is a companion app for the Kashmiri Pandit community: a computed Panchang calendar, festival information, personal reminders, and a Bhajan/Shlok/Aarti library. It is currently in closed beta — features, accuracy, and design may change as we improve it based on tester feedback.

2. Beta software

You're using an early version. Things may occasionally break, look unfinished, or change between updates. Please report anything odd — that's exactly what the beta is for. We do our best to protect your data (especially your reminders) across updates, but back up anything irreplaceable.

3. Accuracy of Panchang and festival information

Wanwun computes tithi, nakshatra, and festival dates using astronomical calculations cross-checked against printed Kashmiri Pandit jantris and established Panchang sources. We take accuracy seriously, but for matters of religious or ceremonial importance, please confirm with your family priest or a trusted local jantri, especially during the beta.
