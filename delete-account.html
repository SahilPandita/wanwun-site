Delete your Wanwun account & data

This page explains how to request deletion, for anyone using Wanwun: Kashmiri Panchang on Android.

Most of your data never leaves your device — your reminders and city selection are stored locally and are deleted the moment you uninstall the app. This page is specifically about the account data created if you chose to sign in with Google.

How to request deletion

  1. Email sahilhost0h@gmail.com from the email address you signed in with, with the subject "Delete my Wanwun account".
  2. We'll confirm your identity via that email address and process the request — typically within 5 business days.
  3. You'll get a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

(An in-app self-service delete button is planned for a future release; email is the supported method during the closed beta.)

What gets deleted

  • Your profile row (name, email, bio, ancestral village/location fields, avatar URL) — stored in our Supabase database.
  • Any feedback messages you submitted through the app.
  • Your Google sign-in link to the app.

What's retained, and for how long

  • Crash and diagnostic logs (Firebase Crashlytics) are not tied to your identity and follow Firebase's standard retention (currently 90 days), after which they're automatically purged. We don't manually delete these on request since they contain no personal data — device model, OS version, and stack traces only.
  • Nothing else is retained after account deletion is processed.

For the full picture of what Wanwun collects and why, see the Privacy Policy.
