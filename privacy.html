Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

Wanwun ("the app", "we", "us") is developed by Sahil Pandita, an individual developer. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and what control you have over it. We wrote it in plain language on purpose.

The short version: Wanwun works fully offline with no account. Signing in with Google is optional; it unlocks profile sync and the Baithak community, where anything you post is public. As of this beta release, we don't show ads or use any analytics SDK, and we never access your device location. Section 2 below explains what may change as the app grows, and how we'll handle that honestly.

1. Data we collect

WhatWhenWhere it's stored
Email, display name, profile photo Only if you choose to sign in with Google Supabase (our backend provider), access-restricted to your account
Bio, general location, ancestral village (optional, self-entered) Only if you fill these in on your Profile Supabase, access-restricted to your account

Personal reminders (title, date, category, notes) Whenever you add a reminder On your device only, in a local database — never uploaded
Crash diagnostics (device model, OS version, stack trace) Only if the app crashes Firebase Crashlytics — contains no personal content

2. Today, and what may come later

As of this beta release:

  • No location tracking. Wanwun never requests GPS or device location. The city you use for Panchang calculations is a manual choice from a list, stored only on your device.
  • No ads. There is no ad SDK anywhere in the app.
  • No analytics or behavioral tracking. We don't use Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or any similar SDK. Crashlytics only activates on an actual crash and reports technical diagnostics, not your activity.
  • No selling of your data. Nothing is sold to data brokers, ever.

To keep Wanwun free and sustainable long-term, a future version may introduce advertising and usage analytics (for example, Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics). If that happens:

  • This policy will be updated first, with a new "Last updated" date, and the change will be described in that app update's release notes — not introduced silently.
  • For users in the EU, UK, and other regions with consent requirements, ads and analytics will only activate after you respond to an in-app consent prompt, as required by Google's own policies.
  • Any advertising identifier or usage data collected will be disclosed here, along with the specific third parties it's shared with and how to opt out (including your device's own "Opt out of Ads Personalization" setting).
  • Your reminders, city selection, and other on-device data described in Section 1 stay exactly as private as they are today — advertising doesn't change how your personal Panchang data is handled.

3. Why we collect what we collect

  • Google sign-in exists so your profile and preferences can follow you across devices, if you want that. The app is fully usable without it.
  • Feedback is stored so we can read, reply to, and act on it — your email is attached only so we can respond if needed.
  • Crash reports help us fix bugs before they affect more people.
  • Community data (Baithak) exists to run the community itself — see the next section.

4. Community content (the Baithak)

The Baithak is Wanwun's community tab. Reading it requires no account. Posting, liking, reporting, and blocking require signing in with Google.

  • What is public: the text of your posts, the date you posted, your display name, and your profile photo. Like counts are public. Anyone using Wanwun can see these — treat a post as public speech, and don't put anything in it you wouldn't want widely seen.
  • What is private: reports you file (the author is never told who reported them — only we can read the report queue) and the list of people you've blocked (visible only to you).
  • Retention: posts and likes are kept for as long as your account exists, or until you delete them. Hidden or removed posts are retained only as long as needed for moderation and legal compliance.
  • Moderation: we may read, hide, or remove community content to enforce our Community Guidelines. Automated checks (a rate limit and a word filter) run on posts as they're submitted.

5. Your control over your data

You can edit or clear your profile fields anytime from the Profile tab. You can delete individual reminders anytime — they never leave your device in the first place.

Deleting your account. Go to Profile → Delete account in the app. This permanently and immediately deletes your account and everything tied to it — your profile, your Baithak posts and likes, your reports and blocks, and your feedback. It cannot be undone. You can also see how to delete your account for the full details.

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you may also request access to, correction of, or erasure of your personal data by writing to sahilhost0h@gmail.com.

6. Third-party services we use

  • Supabase — authentication and database hosting for optional sign-in, profiles, feedback, and the Baithak community.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash diagnostics only.
  • Google Sign-In — used only if you choose to sign in.

7. Children's privacy

Wanwun is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way — including introducing ads or analytics, as described in Section 2 — we'll update the date at the top of this page and, during the beta, let testers know directly.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: sahilhost0h@gmail.com
