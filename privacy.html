Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026

Wanwun ("the app", "we", "us") is developed by Sahil Pandita, an individual developer. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and what control you have over it. We wrote it in plain language on purpose.

The short version: Wanwun works fully offline with no account. Signing in with Google is optional and only unlocks profile sync. We don't show ads, don't use any analytics or tracking SDK, and never access your device location.

1. Data we collect

WhatWhenWhere it's stored
Email, display name, profile photo Only if you choose to sign in with Google Supabase (our backend provider), access-restricted to your account
Bio, general location, ancestral village (optional, self-entered) Only if you fill these in on your Profile Supabase, access-restricted to your account
Feedback messages you submit in-app Only when you use the "Send feedback" form Supabase
Your city selection (for computing your local Panchang) Whenever you pick a city from the in-app list On your device only — never uploaded
Personal reminders (title, date, category, notes) Whenever you add a reminder On your device only, in a local database — never uploaded
Crash diagnostics (device model, OS version, stack trace) Only if the app crashes Firebase Crashlytics — contains no personal content

2. What we deliberately don't do

  • No location tracking. Wanwun never requests GPS or device location. The city you use for Panchang calculations is a manual choice from a list, stored only on your device.
  • No ads. There is no ad SDK anywhere in the app.
  • No analytics or behavioral tracking. We don't use Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or any similar SDK. Crashlytics only activates on an actual crash and reports technical diagnostics, not your activity.
  • No selling or sharing of your data. Nothing is sold, rented, or shared with advertisers, ever.

3. Why we collect what we collect

  • Google sign-in exists so your profile and preferences can follow you across devices, if you want that. The app is fully usable without it.
  • Feedback is stored so we can read, reply to, and act on it — your email is attached only so we can respond if needed.
  • Crash reports help us fix bugs before they affect more people.

4. Your control over your data

You can edit or clear your profile fields anytime from the Profile tab. You can delete individual reminders anytime — they never leave your device in the first place. To request full deletion of your account and any associated Supabase data (profile, feedback), email us at sahilhost0h@gmail.com — we'll confirm once it's done.

5. Third-party services we use

  • Supabase — authentication and database hosting for optional sign-in, profiles, and feedback.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash diagnostics only.
  • Google Sign-In — used only if you choose to sign in.

6. Children's privacy

Wanwun is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we'll update the date at the top of this page and, during the beta, let testers know directly.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: sahilhost0h@gmail.com
