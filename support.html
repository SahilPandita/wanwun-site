Support

We usually reply within a day or two — beta testers even faster.

Fastest way to reach us: use the "Send feedback" form right inside the app (Profile → Feedback) — it comes straight to us with your app version attached, which makes bugs much faster to track down.

Email

For anything else — questions, ideas, partnership inquiries, or if you'd rather not use the in-app form: sahilhost0h@gmail.com

Reporting a bug

The more detail, the faster we can fix it. If you can, include:

  • What you were doing right before it happened
  • What you expected vs. what actually happened
  • Your phone model and Android version, if known
  • A screenshot, if it's a visual issue

Common questions

Why does a tithi or festival date look different from my family's jantri?

Panchang calculations can legitimately vary by a few hours depending on the exact astronomical method and location used. Wanwun computes for your selected city and cross-checks against printed KP jantris. If something looks clearly wrong, please report it — we track down every discrepancy.

I signed in but my profile isn't showing.

Check your internet connection and try pulling to refresh on the Profile tab. If it persists, email us — this is exactly the kind of thing beta testing is for.

Will my reminders survive an app update?

Yes — reminders live in a local database on your device and are designed to survive updates. If you ever see reminders disappear, please report it immediately; we treat that as a top-priority bug.

How do I delete my account/data?

Email sahilhost0h@gmail.com and we'll remove your profile and feedback data from our systems. See the Privacy Policy for details on what's stored where.
