Community Guidelines

Last updated: 14 July 2026

The Baithak is Wanwun's community corner — a shared space for the Kashmiri Pandit community to post announcements, festival notes, and community moments. A baithak is a gathering of neighbours. Please treat it like one.

These guidelines apply to everything you post: text, event notes, and your display name and photo. By posting, you accept them and our Terms of Service.

1. The rules

  • Be respectful. No hate speech, harassment, threats, or personal attacks — including on the basis of religion, caste, region, gender, or origin.
  • No spam or advertising. No promotional content, repetitive posts, scams, or links to unrelated products.
  • Keep it appropriate. No obscene, sexually explicit, violent, or unlawful material.
  • Respect privacy. Don't post other people's personal details — phone numbers, addresses, or photographs of them — without their consent.
  • Don't impersonate. Don't pretend to be someone else, or to speak officially for Wanwun, a temple, or a community organisation.
  • Stay on topic. The Baithak is for the community and its traditions, not for political campaigning or unrelated disputes.
  • Be careful with religious guidance. Share freely, but don't present personal opinion as authoritative ritual instruction.

2. Reporting and blocking

Every post has a ⋮ menu:

  • Report post — flags it for our review. Reports are private; the author isn't told who reported them.
  • Block this person — you immediately stop seeing that person's posts. You can block anyone, for any reason.

3. What happens when content breaks the rules

Depending on severity, we may:

  • hide the post from the Baithak;
  • ask the author to change their behaviour;
  • remove the author's ability to post; or
  • terminate the account.

We may act on a report, or on our own if we notice something. Serious or unlawful content is removed immediately. In an emergency we may temporarily require posts to be approved before they appear.

4. Grievances and takedown requests

If you believe content in the Baithak is unlawful, infringes your rights, or breaks these guidelines, contact us:

Grievance contact: Sahil Pandita — sahilhost0h@gmail.com

In line with India's Information Technology (Intermediary Guidelines and Digital Media Ethics Code) Rules, 2021, we will:

  • acknowledge your complaint within 24 hours; and
  • resolve it within 15 days of receipt.

Please include a link to or description of the content, and why you're reporting it. Content that is unlawful on its face will be taken down as soon as we see it, without waiting for that clock to run.

5. Your content and your account

You own what you write. You keep control of it:

  • Posts you make are public to anyone using Wanwun, along with your display name and profile photo.
  • You can delete your account at any time from Profile → Delete account. That permanently deletes your account and your posts, likes, and reports.

See our Privacy Policy for what we store and for how long.
