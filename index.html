Your Panchang,
computed for you.

Wanwun brings tithi, nakshatra, and festival dates computed for your exact city — not a printed calendar guessing on your behalf. Plus personal reminders and a full Bhajan, Shlok & Aarti library.

Request a beta invite See what's inside

Built like a calendar, not a spreadsheet

Every detail computed, cross-checked, and designed to feel like a sanctuary — not a utility.

🌙

Computed Panchang

Tithi, nakshatra, yoga, karana, and masa — computed on-device via astronomical calculation (Lahiri ayanamsa), not a static yearly table.

📍

Location-aware

Pick your city and every date and time adjusts to it — accurate wherever in the world you are.

🔎

Search that forgives you

Find a day by tithi, date, festival, or reminder — even with typos, transliteration differences, or words in the wrong order.

🎉

Festival accuracy

Festival dates are derived from computed rules, cross-checked against printed KP jantris — traceable, not guessed.

🔔

Personal reminders

Birthdays, anniversaries, pujas — with real local notifications, independent of any account, private to your device.

🪔

Bhajans, Shloks & Aartis

A categorized library with full lyrics and translations, ready whenever you need it.
